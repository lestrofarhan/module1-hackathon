The HiringMine's Journey

What started as a simple initiative to connect job seekers with opportunities through WhatsApp groups has now evolved into a tech-driven, AI-powered career platform. Our vision was clear from day one — to bridge the gap between talented individuals and recruiters by making hiring faster, smarter, and accessible for everyone.
Today, 3,500+ candidates have built professional profiles on HiringMine, and 2,800+ jobs have been shared, helping recruiters discover the right talent with ease. With powerful features like AI-powered resume checker, ATS-based application screening, and instant job posting from text or images, we’re creating a platform where careers grow and companies find their perfect match. And this is just the beginning!
